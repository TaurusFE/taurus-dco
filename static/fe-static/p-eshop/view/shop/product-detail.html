With the exception of the new color Rose Gold, you can not immediately tell the difference between an iPhone 6s and an iPhone 6. But there is a world of difference. You get an incredible camera, many more built-in apps enhancement - and screen 3D Touch (Multi Touch) can detect how hard you press and you'll be able to see and feel that the iPhone 6s respond to your command. Both the iPhone 6s and 6s Plus comes with 3D Touch and Peek and Pop.

12 MP iSight camera. It will not be sharper

iPhone 6s and 12 MP great iSight camera is almost like a professional camera. It focuses on all the details and automatically ensures that the smallest razor sharp. And you can look forward to Live Photo. The new feature automatically captures the moment just before and just after you press the shutter button. Both the iPhone 6s and 6s Plus is upgraded to iOS 9 - the currently most advanced, intelligent and secure operating system. iOS9 combined with the strong A9 processor with 64-bit architecture ensures that the iPhone 6s responds instantly and doing graphics-intensive apps as easy as easily nothing.

Vælger du betaling med betalingskort, er der ingen betalingsgebyrer på din regning.
